# Wild Curiosity — Brand System
## READ THIS BEFORE WRITING ANY CODE OR DESIGNING ANYTHING

---

## What the business is

Wild Curiosity is an AI visibility consultancy for independent luxury hotels in Europe.
We scan how AI engines (ChatGPT, Perplexity, Gemini, Claude etc.) describe hotels and fix the ones that are invisible.

- Clients: 4–5 star, owner-operated hotels
- Minimum engagement: €3,000/month
- Founder: Asha Cowell — hello@wildcuriosity.ai
- Website: wildcuriosity.ai

---

## Tone of voice

- British English always
- Specific over general — never vague
- No buzzwords. No "leverage", "synergy", "unlock potential"
- Confident, warm, direct
- Good copy: "1881 Ernst Ziller landmark. Not once mentioned by any AI engine."
- Bad copy: "We help hotels maximise their digital presence across AI platforms."

---

## Fonts — 2 ONLY. Never add a third.

1. Bodoni Moda — headlines, hotel names, subheadings, body italic, logo
2. Space Mono — all data: coordinates, scores, labels, pills, nav links, metadata

Never use Inter, Roboto, Arial, or any sans-serif other than Space Mono.

---

## Colour Palette — CONFIRMED. Do not change hex values.

- Cream:      #EDE6D0  — primary text on dark, light section backgrounds
- Ocean Blue: #9DBFC4  — primary accent, Invisible status, WILD in logo
- Avocado:    #B5AF45  — secondary accent, Barely Visible status
- Olive:      #45441A  — tertiary depth
- Dark Green: #1A1F0E  — primary background
- Ink:        #0E1009  — deepest background, nav, footer

---

## Logo — CONFIRMED. Do not modify under any circumstances.

Wordmark:
- WILD — Bodoni Moda weight 900, all caps, Ocean Blue #9DBFC4
- CURIOSITY — Bodoni Moda italic weight 700, all caps, Cream #EDE6D0
- Stacked vertically, WILD on top, CURIOSITY below
- No tagline in the logo itself
- No additional graphic elements

Nav size:
- WILD: 22px, letter-spacing -0.01em
- CURIOSITY: 16px italic, letter-spacing 0.06em

1. Nav — Ink #0E1009
2. Hero — Dark Green #1A1F0E, pin on right panel
3. Colour strip — 5 palette swatches, 6px tall
4. What We Do — Cream #EDE6D0, dark text
5. Colour strip — reversed
6. Prospects/Cards — Dark Green #1A1F0E
7. Colour strip
8. How It Works — Cream #EDE6D0, dark text
9. Footer — Ink #0E1009

---

## What to avoid

- Purple gradients, glassmorphism, generic SaaS blue
- Any text smaller than 10px
- Three or more fonts
- Changing the logo
- The word "Europe" in subheadings
- Anything that looks like a hotel booking platform
