02. The Shift

Travel discovery has changed.

For years, travellers searched Google and compared dozens of options. Now they ask AI. And AI recommends a handful of hotels it understands best — often the same names across Amalfi, Lake Como, Santorini.

If your property isn't one of them, it's invisible. The fix is not more marketing. It's telling your story in language AI can use.

Generic

Luxury boutique hotel in Positano with sea views. Modern amenities. Excellent service.

Rewritten for AI

Family-run since 1962, perched where the Amalfi road meets the sea. Twelve rooms, hand-painted tiles from the owner's workshop.
