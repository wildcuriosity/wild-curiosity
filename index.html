The Problem

Your competitors aren’t better than you. They’re just easier for AI to describe.

AI does not read booking platforms. It builds its understanding from structured content across the open web, and most independent hotels were never set up for that.

01

Not built for how AI learns

AI learns from your website, destination guides, and editorial coverage. Not your TripAdvisor rating.

02

Beautiful sites, little structure

Many boutique hotel websites look beautiful. However they often give AI very little structured information to work with.

03

Algorithms fall back on familiarity

If AI is unsure, it recommends names it already knows. That visibility gap is solvable.
